Site Name: Guatemala City Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose:

The Guatemala City Chamber of Commerce website will provide you with the most recent information of the Guatemala City community, including current events, it will also provide you with the business spotlights and how you can join to our Chamber of commerce.

Scenarios

How can I join this chamber of commerce?

What are the events that the chamber of commerce will be promoting in our community?

What are the latest news of our community regarding this chamber of commerce?

Color Schema

The royal blue color will be used on the header and on the footer

The sky blue color will be used on the navigation, and in some borders for the content of the webpage.

The font color will be black

Most of the background will be white

Typography

Font Oswald will be used for the titles and the header

Font Poppins will be used for the paragraphs and navigation
